Family Life Ministry

Family Life Ministry is Word For All Nation’s ministry that serves families — teaching families to have powerhouse Christian homes, producing Godly parents who can train children the way God wants them to go and encouraging others to engage better by Godly principles before entering marriage.

Our Goals

It is the Family Life Ministry’s goal for your home to be that strong powerhouse home, an oasis of peace to the lost and a beacon to those seeking answers.
