Kingdom Kids ministry

Kingdom Kids Ministry under Word for All Nations is established to equip children ages 3 to 12 in the ways of God. It aims to train them young (Proverbs 22:6 NKJV) to be grounded in the Word of God.

Children of God

KKM wants to establish their relationship with Jesus and identity in Christ and be strong in the Spirit of God so that when they grow up, they will become part of godly generation.
