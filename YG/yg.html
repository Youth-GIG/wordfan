What is YouthGiG

YouthGig is a youth organization that comes together to share Jesus to a group of young people who have a common goal of spreading the teachings and gospel of Jesus Christ. We are an organizations that meet together F2F every Sunday to learn and study His grace. We also conduct once a month online youth convergence to meet up with other youth group in our church.

Join YouthGiG
and be part of a group

Kairos

Ages: 13-17

"Kairos" refers to a moment or season of the youth where they need to understand and discover their true Identity in Christ. "Kairos Youth Group" is ages 13-17 years old youth with a faith-based group that is focused on helping young people recognize and seize the opportunities for spiritual growth and Identity development that Christ gave at the cross for those who will believe and received Him.

Mathetes

Ages: 18-25

focuses on helping and training young people grow in their faith, develop their gifts and become disciples of Jesus. that often provide opportunities for youth to participate in worship services, Bible study groups, mission trips, service projects, and social events, all with the goal of helping other young people deepen their relationship with Jesus

BenGen

Ages: 25-30

Bengen Group: Is a young professional group ages 25 - 30 . These is a youth group that focused on religious or spiritual development, community service supports, leadership development, or personal carreer growth.
