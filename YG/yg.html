YouthGig is the youth and young adults ministry of Word for All Nations. We come together with the common goal of sharing Jesus to the younger generation. Our focus is to provide a community for young people to grow in their faith and knowledge of God, to develop strong relationships with one another, and to share the love of Jesus with those around them. We do this by holding weekly Sunday meetings to talk about Jesus and His grace plus a once-a-month online youth convergence to meet with other youth groups from other churches[a]. Our meetings, camps and activities are aimed at educating, inspiring, and engaging young people in their Christian faith. Through these interactions, we aim to help the younger generation live out their faith and share it with others, make a positive impact on our nation, and help build a stronger, more impactful loving Christian community of the world.
