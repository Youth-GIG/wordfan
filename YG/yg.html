YouthGig is a youth organization that comes together to share Jesus to a group of young people who have a common goal of spreading the teachings and gospel of Jesus Christ. We are an organizations that meet together F2F every Sunday to learn and study His grace. We also conduct once a month online youth convergence to meet up with other youth group in our church. We hold events, camps and activities, that aimed at educating, inspiring, and engaging young people in their Christian faith. These can range from youth services and prayer gatherings to community service projects, retreats, and conferences. The focus of these organizations is typically to provide a supportive community for young people to grow in their faith and knowledge of His love, develop strong relationships with one another, and share the love of Jesus with those around them. Through their activities and interactions, members of these organizations aim to live out their faith and share it with others, making a positive impact on our nation and helping to build a stronger, more impactful loving Christian community of the world.
